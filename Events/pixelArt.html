Pixel Art Competition

Overview

A digital art competition where participants create pixel-based artworks using limited grids and color palettes. Focus on creativity and technique as you produce original artwork within the given time.

Rules and Regulations

Participation Format:

  • This event is open to individual participants only.
  • Participants must bring their own laptop.

Design Guidelines:

  • The theme will be announced on the spot during the event.
  • Allowed Software includes Aseprite, Piskel, Photoshop, or manual pixel grid methods.
  • No AI-generated art or auto-pixelization tools are permitted.
  • The artwork should be created on an A4-sized canvas.
  • The time limit for submission is 3 hours.

Winning and Scoring Criteria

  • Creativity and originality in using pixel art techniques.
  • Attention to detail and use of color palettes.
  • Adherence to the given theme and guidelines.
  • Overall aesthetic appeal and presentation of the artwork.
