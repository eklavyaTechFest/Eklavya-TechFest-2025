Cultural Events

Singing Competition

A melodic showdown where participants showcase their vocal talent in solo or group performances. Performances may span various genres and languages, with provisions for backing tracks or live instruments.

Rules and Regulations - Singing

  • Open to solo singers and groups.
  • Time limit: 3-5 minutes per performance.
  • Backing tracks must be submitted prior to the performance.
  • Live accompaniment is allowed; participants must bring their own instruments if required.
  • Judging Criteria: Vocal quality (pitch, tone, and control), stage presence, and song selection.

Dance Competition

A high-energy competition where dancers captivate the audience with their moves, expressions, and choreography. Performances can be solo, duet, or group acts.

Rules and Regulations - Dancing

  • Open to solo, duet, and group performances.
  • Performance time: 3-5 minutes for solos and 5-8 minutes for group acts.
  • Participants must bring their own props and costumes.
  • Music tracks must be submitted before the performance.
  • Judging Criteria: Choreography and execution, expression and stage presence, and synchronization (for group performances).
