Robo War

Overview

A high-octane battle where teams pit their custom-built robot "cars" against one another in a specially designed arena with obstacles and hazards.

Rules and Regulations

Robot Specifications:

  • Dimensions: Maximum length: 30 cm, Maximum width: 25 cm.
  • Robot Type: Only self-made robots are allowed. Purchased robots are not included.

Team Guidelines:

  • Both individuals and teams can participate in RoboMaze at Techfest.
  • A team can have a maximum of three participants.
  • Team members must be from the same institute.
  • Each participant must have a unique name. Techfest organizers reserve the right to reject entries with inappropriate or offensive names unless changed.
  • If participating as a team, a team representative (leader) must be specified, along with their contact details (phone number, email ID, etc.) at the time of registration.

Winning and Scoring Criteria

  • To win the competition, participants must pass each checkpoint inside the maze, earning points.
  • In case of a tie (same points), the winner will be decided based on the completion time.
  • The winner will be determined by following the given instructions.
