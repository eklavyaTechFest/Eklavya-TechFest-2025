BGMI

Rules and Regulations

Match Format:

  • Semi-Final (Day 1): Each player/team plays 4 matches; top 15 teams qualify for the finals.
  • Final (Day 2): Top 4 teams advance to a final round featuring a Team Deathmatch (TDM) showdown.

Point System:

  • Placement Points: 1st – 10 points, 2nd – 6 points, 3rd – 4 points, 4th – 3 points, 5th – 2 points, 6th to 9th – 1 point each.
  • Kill Points: 1 point for every kill.
  • MVP Award: Given to the player with the most kills in one match.

Team Guidelines:

  • Both individuals and teams can participate.
  • A team can have a maximum of 4 participants.
  • All team members must be from the same institute.
  • Each team must have a unique name; inappropriate names may be rejected.
  • If participating as a team, a team representative must be designated with valid contact details during registration.

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2)

Rules and Regulations

Match Format:

  • Players are randomly assigned into teams of 5 for a 5v5 Team Deathmatch (TDM).
  • Each team consists of 5 players.
  • Teams compete against each other in the preliminary rounds.
  • The winning teams advance to a Deathmatch Round where individual players compete 1v1.
  • The top 3 players from the Deathmatch Round are shortlisted for a final 1v1 battle.
  • The last standing player in the final round is declared the winner.

Point System:

  • Advancement is determined by match victories and individual performance in the Deathmatch Round.

Player Guidelines:

  • Only individuals can register.
  • Each player must have a unique name; inappropriate names may be rejected.
  • Player with valid contact details must be designated during registration.

Asphalt Legends Unite

Rules and Regulations

Match Format:

  • Each match consists of 4 to 6 players.
  • A total of 8 games will be hosted.
  • In each game, the top player qualifies for the finals.
  • Finals: The 8 qualifiers compete in one final race. The winner is declared the champion, and the player with the most knockdowns receives additional perks.

Point System:

  • Advancement is based solely on placement; no additional point system is applied.

Player Guidelines:

  • Only individuals can participate.
  • Each player must have a unique name; inappropriate names may be rejected.
  • Player with valid contact details must be designated during registration.

Winning and Scoring Criteria

  • The overall winner is determined by cumulative performance and match victories.
  • MVP and Impact Player Awards are given based on exceptional performance.

Prizes

  • Cash Prizes, Certificates, and Medals
