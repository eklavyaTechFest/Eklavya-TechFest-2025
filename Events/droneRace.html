Drone Race

Overview

A high-speed aerial competition where participants pilot drones through a challenging course filled with checkpoints and obstacles. Precision, control, and quick decision-making are key to conquering this race.

Rules and Regulations

Drone Specifications:

  • Type: Both self-made and purchased FPV drones are allowed.
  • Safety Feature: Drones must have a mandatory power-off mechanism that shuts them down within 60 seconds.
  • Control: Manual or FPV (First-Person View) flying is permitted.

Race Format:

  • Participants are divided into two groups (Group A & Group B).
  • Internal matches determine the finalists within each group.
  • The winner is the participant who completes the course first while passing all checkpoints.
  • Penalties apply for missing checkpoints or crashing.

Team Guidelines:

  • Both individuals and teams can participate.
  • Teams (if participating) can have a maximum of four participants.
  • All team members must be from the same institute.
  • Each team must have a unique name; inappropriate names may be rejected.
  • If participating as a team, a team representative must be designated with valid contact details during registration.

Winning and Scoring Criteria

  • The winner is determined by the fastest completion time while clearing all checkpoints.
  • Penalties or time deductions may be applied for any missed checkpoints or infractions.
