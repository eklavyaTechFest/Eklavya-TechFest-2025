Digital Art Competition

Overview

↓ ↓   Register   ↓ ↓

A digital painting and illustration contest where participants showcase their creativity and artistic skills using digital tools. Compete individually by creating original artwork within a limited time.

← →

Rules and Regulations

23/04/2025

Participation Format:

  • This event is open to individual participants only.
  • Participants must bring their own digital tablet or laptop.

Design Guidelines:

  • The theme will be announced on the spot during the event.
  • Allowed Software: Photoshop, Procreate, Krita, or any digital painting software.
  • No AI-generated art or auto-enhancement tools are allowed.
  • The final artwork must be submitted in JPEG or PNG format (high resolution, minimum 300 DPI).
  • The time limit for final submission is 3 hours.

Winning and Scoring Criteria

  • Creativity and originality of the artwork.
  • Technical skill and overall aesthetic appeal.
  • Adherence to the theme and guidelines.
  • Presentation and clarity of the concept behind the artwork.

Prizes

  • Cash Prize, Certificates, Medals

Venue

Boys Common Room (Room No. 017), New Academic Building

Note

Participation in other events:

  • If you are taking part in Digital Art then you will not able to participate in Hackathon, Ui/Ux desinging, CTF, Fast Calculation, Gaming(Bgmi and Asphalt).
